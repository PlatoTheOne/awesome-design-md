Typography Scale

Display stays wide and decisive. Mono is for utility labels and technical notes, not expressive headlines.

Motion Principles

Motion should explain state changes and sequence, not act as decorative noise. Reduced-motion preference is respected.

Lateral motion for transfer progress and direction.

Pulse for focus or live state acknowledgement.

Vertical shift for hierarchy reveal and inline transition.
