Everyday vs Expressive

The source system separates stable product surfaces from expressive campaign treatment. Both belong, but not in the same intensity.

Everyday Usage

Product surfaces stay steady.

Use neutral backgrounds, direct labels, and clear action hierarchy. The goal is confidence and control.

Expressive Usage

Campaign surfaces can push.

Use one strong accent family, larger display type, and more dramatic contrast while keeping key information readable.
